# Ernie

A premium, offline-first spaced repetition flashcard app for serious learners.

## What is Ernie?

Ernie is a native mobile flashcard app built for people who actually need to remember things — board exam preppers, med students, language learners. It uses FSRS-5, the most accurate spaced repetition algorithm available, to schedule your reviews at the optimal time.

## Why Ernie?

- **Fully offline.** No accounts, no cloud, no internet required. Your data stays on your device.
- **One-time purchase.** No subscriptions, no ads, no telemetry. Pay once, own it forever.
- **Real spaced repetition.** FSRS-5 scheduling with interval previews on every rating button — you see exactly when you'll review each card next.
- **Daily backups.** Automatic backups to your own cloud storage (iCloud, Google Drive, or Files). Your data, your control.
- **Fast card creation.** Go from "I need to remember this" to a saved card in under 30 seconds.

## Card Types

- **Basic** — front/back question and answer
- **Cloze** — fill-in-the-blank with multiple deletions
- **Image Occlusion** — hide parts of a diagram or image

## Platforms
